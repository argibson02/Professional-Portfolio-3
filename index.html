About Me

My name is Alex Gibson, and welcome to my webpage! For the past five years, I have been working in software Quality Assurance and have decided to make the shift from testing code, to actually writing it. I am currently training to become a full-stack developer at the University of California - Berkeley Extension and hope to graduate in December 2021 with a Certificate of Course Completion. I have experience working with Javascript, jQuery, HTML, CSS, Materialize, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express.js, SQL, handlebars.js, and various Web APIs.

I am an experienced Quality Assurance Analyst with a demonstrated history of success in the electronic medical records and software industry. I have worn many hats in my role and have done things including: managing software development projects and quality, implementing process improvements, championing accessibility and internationalization testing, and advocating for the end-user experience.

The 3D Art Gallery

The 3D Art Gallery project aims to improve upon the virtual museum experience by allowing users to interact and get up close to paintings in a 3D space. Users can grab, manipulate, zoom in, zoom out, and see how light reflects off the layers and cracks on a piece of art.

Moonbase

Moonbase is a dedicated investment chat forum with integrated market data to provide investors a one-stop-shop for talking about and viewing stocks. Users can browse and create posts, comment, “hype up” posts, and see trending and individual stock data all from within the Moonbase website.

Team Profile Generator

The Team Profile Generator is a Futurama-themed HTML webpage generation tool powered by Node.js and Inquirer. Users can input their team members' info into a command prompt and auto-generate a team profile page.
